Phase 2 — The crime scene

What happened?

It is a grey Tuesday morning on the shores of Loch Ness. When local rangers arrive for their daily check, something is wrong — Nessie is gone. The creature's usual area is empty, the water is calm, and there are no signs of a struggle. But there are footprints leading from a nearby car park to the the lake, and a small boat is missing from the boat dock.

By midday, the boat has returned — but nobody saw who was driving it. A local journalist writes: "Nessie has vanished. Was it an accident — or something more deliberate?"

The rangers call in a team of young investigators. That's you.
